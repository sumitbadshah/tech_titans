> **Stop impulse buying. Start mindful spending.**  
> A Chrome extension that introduces intentional friction into the online checkout process, helping young professionals build healthier financial habits.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🛒 Smart Checkout Detection
- Automatically detects product pages on **Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Meesho, and Shopify stores**
- Captures product title, price, and platform

### ⏱️ Cooling-Off Period
- Blocks immediate purchase with an animated countdown ring
- Configurable delay: 1–60 minutes
- Proceed button disabled until timer completes

### 💰 True Cost in Work Hours
- Shows product price ÷ your hourly wage
- **"This purchase costs you X hours of work"**
- Puts spending in perspective

### 🤔 Reflective Nudge Questions
- 10 rotating mindful prompts during cooldown:
  - *"Do I really need this?"*
  - *"Will this bring lasting happiness?"*
  - *"Can this money be better spent?"*

### 📊 Analytics Dashboard
- Track money saved and hours saved
- Visualize spending patterns with charts
- View recent purchase decisions (completed/cancelled)

### 🔒 Privacy First
- Local storage with optional cloud sync
- No tracking, no ads, no data selling

---

## 🎯 How It Works
User visits Amazon/Flipkart/Myntra
↓

Extension detects product price
↓

Shows cost in working hours + 30-second cooldown
↓

User reflects on rotating mindful prompts
↓

After cooldown: Proceed or Cancel
↓

Decision logged → Analytics update
